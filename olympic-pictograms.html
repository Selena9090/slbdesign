Olympic Pictograms

Description of Project:

The events that I chose to make into pictograms were, basketball, football, tennis, and volleyball from the Summer Olympics list. I had got inspiration from the human muscel anatomy and thought that it would best showcase each sport and design it in an abstract way.

Client: Emily Moody
