Book Trilogy

Description of Project:

Criminal by Pheobe Judge, is a podcast about people who have done wrong, been wronged, or maybe have gotten caught somewhere in the middle. Besides listening to her podcast, what if you were tasked to create trilogy book covers of the stories from Pheobe Judge? After doing a lot of research, listening to multiple podcasts, and endless sketches later, these are the final results of the project.

Client: Emily Moody
Service: Visual Identity Design, Graphic Design
Skills: Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator
